SELLER AGREEMENT

Seller Policy Agreement

Effective from the date you tick "I agree" in the app.

By accepting this agreement, you ("Seller") agree to sell digital creative assets through the Kreate Studio mobile application ("Platform", "Kreate Studio") under the terms set out here.

1. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and capable of entering into a legally binding agreement under Indian law. You must hold a valid UPI ID linked to a bank account in your own name — payouts are only ever sent to the UPI ID registered on your account.

2. What you're allowed to sell

You may list original digital creative assets that you have created, or that you hold full legal rights and licenses to redistribute — including UI kits, code templates, motion graphics, audio packs, logo kits, Figma files, Flutter templates, and other categories shown in the Upload screen.

You may not upload:

  • Content you do not own or do not have explicit rights to redistribute, including pirated or stolen assets
  • Malware, viruses, scripts, or any file designed to harm a buyer's device or system
  • Content that infringes another party's copyright, trademark, or intellectual property
  • Sexually explicit, violent, hateful, or illegal content
  • Misleading listings — fake previews, false descriptions, or assets that don't match what's shown

3. Listing review and content inspection

All listings are reviewed by the Kreate Studio team before going live. Review typically completes within 24 hours of submission.

During review, our team may:

  • Inspect your preview images and listing description
  • Open and examine the actual uploaded file content — not just its name, type, or size — to verify it matches your listing description and complies with this policy
  • Approve the listing (it goes live immediately) or reject it with a reason shown in your app

If a listing is rejected, you can edit it and resubmit for review. If a listing is found to violate this policy after approval (e.g. reported by a buyer), we reserve the right to remove it — with or without prior notice — and may suspend the associated account for repeated or serious violations.

Editing a listing's preview images or asset file after approval will send it back for re-review. Editing only text fields (title, description, price, category) does not require re-review.

4. Pricing and platform fee

You set your own listing price, between ₹0 (free) and ₹50,000. Buyers pay your listed price plus a combined platform and payment-processing fee of 12.5%, charged on top of your price — you are not charged this fee, and you receive 100% of the price you list.

Example: if you list an asset at ₹1,000, the buyer pays ₹1,125. You receive the full ₹1,000.

5. How payouts work

Kreate Studio is in its early stage, and payouts currently work as follows:

  • When a buyer purchases your listing, Razorpay processes the payment and settles funds to Kreate Studio's account, typically within 2 business days.
  • Kreate Studio then transfers your earnings to the UPI ID on your account manually.
  • We aim to complete payouts within 7 business days of fund settlement, though this may take longer during high-volume periods.
  • You can track payout status anytime from your Seller Dashboard in the app — "Pending" means funds haven't been sent yet, "Paid" means they have.
  • You will receive an in-app notification when your listing is approved, rejected, or when a payout is marked as sent.

This manual process is temporary while we work toward fully automated payouts. We'll notify all sellers in-app when that changes.

You are responsible for keeping your UPI ID accurate and up to date. Kreate Studio is not liable for payouts sent to an incorrect UPI ID that you provided.

6. Editing and removing listings

You can edit a listing's title, description, price, category, and preview images at any time. Editing preview images or the asset file sends the listing back for re-review and temporarily hides it from buyers. Editing text fields (title, description, price, category) updates the listing instantly without re-review, regardless of whether purchases exist.

Note: a buyer's existing purchase record always reflects the price they originally paid — changing your price does not affect past transactions. You may delete your own listing at any time; buyers who already purchased it keep access to their download.

7. Refunds and disputes

Digital downloads are generally non-refundable once delivered, since a file can't be "returned." That said, if a buyer reports a broken file, a listing that doesn't match its description, or suspected fraud, Kreate Studio may investigate and, where warranted, withhold or reverse a payout pending resolution. We act as a neutral platform facilitating the transaction between you and the buyer — we are not a party to it.

8. Taxes

You are solely responsible for any GST, income tax, or other tax obligations arising from your sales on Kreate Studio. We do not currently withhold or remit taxes on your behalf.

9. Account suspension

We may suspend or terminate your seller account for violating this policy, fraudulent activity, repeated verified buyer complaints, or misuse of the platform. Where possible, we'll explain why.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the platform grows. If we make a material change, we'll ask you to re-accept it before you can publish new listings. Continuing to sell after a minor update means you accept the revised terms.

11. Limitation of liability
