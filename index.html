0x01◇ BLOCK · INTRODUCTION

Introduction

Welcome to Kreate Studio ("we", "our", or "us"), operated by Ansh Bhardwaj. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Kreate Studio mobile application ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

0x02◇ BLOCK · DATA INPUTS

Information We Collect

01 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Role (Buyer or Seller)
  • UPI ID (for Sellers only)

02 Content You Upload

When you upload listings as a Seller, we collect:

  • Listing title and description
  • Preview images
  • Asset files
  • Pricing information

03 Transaction Information

When purchases are made, we collect:

  • Payment details (processed by Razorpay)
  • Purchase history
  • Download records

04 Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device information
  • App usage data
  • Search queries
  • Interaction data
0x03◇ BLOCK · PROCESSING

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Enable buying and selling of creative assets
  • Send verification and transactional emails
  • Improve our App and services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations
0x04◇ BLOCK · PAYMENTS

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not store your payment card details. Razorpay's Privacy Policy applies to payment processing. UPI IDs provided by Sellers are used solely for receiving payments via Razorpay Route.

0x05◇ BLOCK · STORAGE

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google) services:

  • Firebase Authentication — account security
  • Firebase Realtime Database — app data
  • Firebase Storage — files and images

Google's Privacy Policy applies to data stored on Firebase services.

0x06◇ BLOCK · SHARING

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing
  • Firebase (Google) — for data storage and authentication
  • Law enforcement — when required by law
0x07◇ BLOCK · USER RIGHTS

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: workwithansh8@gmail.com

0x08◇ BLOCK · DELETION

Account Deletion

You can delete your account from the App settings. Upon deletion:

  • Your profile is permanently removed
  • Your listings are removed from the marketplace
  • Transaction records may be retained for legal compliance
0x09◇ BLOCK · MINORS

Children's Privacy

Kreate Studio is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

0x0A◇ BLOCK · SECURITY

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data including:

  • Email verification for all accounts
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Firebase security rules
  • Encrypted data transmission
0x0B◇ BLOCK · AMENDMENTS

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
